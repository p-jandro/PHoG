## TRUE / FALSE

40 statements. Each has the boolean answer and a short explanation.

1. **Ferenc Puskás scored 84 goals in 85 official matches for the Hungarian national team.** → **TRUE.** One of the best international scoring ratios ever.

2. **The Puskás Aréna in Budapest will host the 2026 UEFA Champions League final.** → **TRUE.** Awarded by UEFA in 2023.

3. **Hungary won the 1954 FIFA World Cup.** → **FALSE.** Hungary lost the final 3–2 to West Germany in Bern — the "Miracle of Bern."

4. **Flórián Albert is the only Hungarian to have won the Ballon d'Or.** → **TRUE.** Won in 1967.

5. **Ferencváros's 7-in-a-row run of NB1 titles from 2018–19 to 2024–25 makes 36 championships in total for the club.** → **TRUE.** As of 2024–25.

6. **Michael Schumacher and Lewis Hamilton are tied with 7 Formula 1 Drivers' titles each.** → **TRUE.**

7. **Lando Norris won the 2025 Formula 1 Drivers' Championship.** → **TRUE.**

8. **Sebastian Vettel is still the youngest Formula 1 World Champion in history.** → **TRUE.** 23 years, 134 days in 2010.

9. **The Hungaroring is sometimes nicknamed "Monaco without the barriers."** → **TRUE.** Narrow, twisty layout in a natural amphitheatre.

10. **Ayrton Senna died at the Belgian Grand Prix in 1994.** → **FALSE.** He died at the San Marino GP at Imola.

11. **F1 introduced the Halo cockpit-protection device in 2018.** → **TRUE.** Mandatory across FIA single-seaters from that year.

12. **The shortest race in F1 history was the 2021 Belgian Grand Prix at Spa — basically two laps behind the safety car.** → **TRUE.** Half points were awarded.

13. **Russia and China each share land borders with 14 countries — the joint world record.** → **TRUE.**

14. **Hungary borders exactly seven other countries.** → **TRUE.** Austria, Slovakia, Ukraine, Romania, Serbia, Croatia, Slovenia.

15. **There are exactly three doubly landlocked countries in the world.** → **FALSE.** There are two: Liechtenstein and Uzbekistan.

16. **Lake Baikal contains roughly 22% of all unfrozen surface freshwater on Earth.** → **TRUE.**

17. **France has the most time zones of any country in the world if you include its overseas territories.** → **TRUE.** 12 zones.

18. **Nepal uses a time offset of UTC+5:45 — one of the world's only 45-minute offsets.** → **TRUE.**

19. **Both San Marino and Vatican City are entirely surrounded by Italy.** → **TRUE.**

20. **Trackmania Nations Forever was released free in 2008.** → **TRUE.** On 16 April 2008.

21. **Trackmania Nations Forever has all seven environments of the Trackmania universe in it.** → **FALSE.** Only Stadium. The 7-environment game is Trackmania United Forever.

22. **In Trackmania, Author Medal (green) means you matched or beat the map-maker's own time.** → **TRUE.**

23. **The 2022 ZrT Trackmania Cup final was held at the Bercy Accor Arena in Paris in front of around 15,000 spectators.** → **TRUE.**

24. **The Wirtual YouTube channel is largely responsible for Trackmania going mainstream in the English-speaking world from around 2019.** → **TRUE.**

25. **Monopoly was originally designed in 1903 by a woman named Elizabeth Magie.** → **TRUE.** She called it "The Landlord's Game."

26. **The "Free Parking jackpot" — where all paid fines collect in the middle and the lander wins them — is an official Monopoly rule.** → **FALSE.** It's a house rule. Officially, nothing happens on Free Parking.

27. **In Bang!, the Sheriff starts the game with one more life than other characters of the same type.** → **TRUE.** Sheriff gets +1 max life.

28. **In standard Perudo, 1s are wild and count for any face.** → **TRUE.** Except during "Palifico" rounds.

29. **Hitster requires a Spotify account to scan and play song cards.** → **TRUE.**

30. **In Hitster you win the game by being the first player to correctly place 10 song cards on your personal timeline.** → **TRUE.**

31. **The first underground railway in continental Europe opened in Budapest in 1896.** → **TRUE.** The Millennium Underground (today's M1).

32. **The Chain Bridge (Széchenyi Lánchíd) was the first permanent bridge across the Danube anywhere in Hungary.** → **TRUE.** Opened 1849.

33. **Debrecen was a temporary capital of Hungary in both 1849 and 1944.** → **TRUE.**

34. **Hungary's largest stadium by capacity is the Groupama Aréna.** → **FALSE.** The Puskás Aréna (~67,200) is much larger.

35. **The Miskolctapolca Cave Bath is heated water inside a natural karst cave.** → **TRUE.** ~30 °C, low mineral content.

36. **Lake Tisza is a natural lake formed by glacial activity.** → **FALSE.** It's an artificial reservoir created by the Kisköre Dam (completed 1973).

37. **The town of Kisköre is home to Hungary's largest hydroelectric power plant.** → **TRUE.** ~28 MW with bulb/pipe turbines.

38. **Diósgyőr Castle in Miskolc is nicknamed the "Castle of the Queens" because it was traditionally given as a wedding gift to Hungarian queens.** → **TRUE.**

39. **Balaton Sound was held every year from 2007 until at least 2024.** → **TRUE.** The festival paused starting 2025.

40. **The Szántód–Tihany car ferry is the shortest ferry crossing on Lake Balaton.** → **TRUE.**

## NOTES / CORRECTIONS WORTH FLAGGING

- **Hitster designer**: the brief said "Sebastian Engel"; every source credits **Marcus Carleson** (Swedish). Used in Round 10 and T/F 30-context.
- **Lake Tisza name**: "Lake Tisza" and "Kisköre Reservoir" are the same body of water. Round P6 keeps Lake Balaton distinct.
- **TMNF environments**: TMNF is single-environment (Stadium). The "name any environment" Pointless round (P4) is therefore really a *franchise-wide* question, not TMNF-specific — worth a category rename if used.
- **Dzsudzsák in P8**: His biggest career moves were Anzhi (Russia) and Bursa (Turkey), which aren't top-5 leagues. Consider swapping in **László Bénes** (Borussia Mönchengladbach) or removing.
- **Bang! role count**: the base game has the four roles listed. A fifth "Vice-Sheriff" appears only in some translations as a synonym for Deputy — don't accept it.
- **Pointless scores are estimates.** Real scores would come from surveying ~100 Hungarian 30-somethings. Adjust freely on playtest.
